Case-Based Reasoning (CBR) and Automated Case Elicitation (ACE):
CBR systems learn from past experiences (cases) to solve new problems. 
ACE is a methodology within CBR that allows a system to automatically acquire knowledge by exploring an environment and interacting with it, rather than requiring pre-coded knowledge. 
Knowledge Graphs:
These structured representations of domain knowledge connect entities and relationships. 
Techniques exist to automatically identify relevant knowledge graphs or specific portions of cross-domain knowledge graphs that represent a particular domain.
