About the Project

AngioVision

AngioVision is a research project at UC Davis exploring multi-modal analysis of coronary angiography — pairing angiographic image sequences with their radiology reports and applying modern machine learning, vision-language models, and retrieval-augmented generation to surface clinically meaningful insight. This query engine lets you explore the curated DICOM database in natural language and find visually similar cases through image-based vector search.
